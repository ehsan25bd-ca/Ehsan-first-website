'PFLO' PROGRAM (To Solve Energy Equation)

The'PFLO'program solves the energy equation for up to two pipes in series using the Darcy-Weisbach friction formula.

To calculate 'd2', first assume a value of d2 ( say, same as d1) and calculate the friction factors. Then solve the energy equation for 'd2' to get the required diameter of the pipe.
